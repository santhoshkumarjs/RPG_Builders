About us

OUR PROFILE

RPG Contractors and Builders was established in the year 2017. We have a team of experienced directors and dynamic professionals who have a sole motive to grow in the field of real estate and create a benchmark for us. The portfolio of activities consists of building homes and contributing largely to the real estate development in Chennai. Over a decade of experience in the real estate field, we have been constantly trying to serve the citizens by providing residential and commercial spaces in sustainable quality standards for their happy living at the best affordable prices.

We strive for excellence in a wave of newer technology, innovation, safety standards and the quest for growth are inimitable with timely execution that is in line with the highest living standards.

VISION

We create a standard of our service in terms of people, place & Passion to be the best in the industry.

MISSION

To provide creative solutions, by customizing our services to suit the requirements of our clients. To encourage & facilitate our team to reach its best potential combining its diverse strengths to provide total customer satisfaction.

background

We started as a journey by a single individual with a desire has now become an organization which is serving the needs of the real estate industry in our society today. We have a good partnership, reputed clients all over Tamilnadu.

RPG Contractors & Builders Pvt. Ltd., Has the best talented individuals on its board of directors who are professionals in real estate, construction, finance & accounting sector providing the best of services to our clients through value added services to their properties through our team of experts.
